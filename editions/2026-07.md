## Model Serving — Speculative Decoding Goes Default

### Tier A roster
No changes from June 2026.

### Findings
- **SGLang v0.5.11** released (May 2026): Paired with RadixArk public launch and XGrammar-2. Includes security fixes for CVE-2026-3059 and CVE-2026-3060.
- **vLLM V1 architecture** is the major evolution: torch.compile integration, KVConnector for prefill/decode disaggregation, optimization levels (`-O0` to `-O2`).
- **Speculative decoding is now production-default**: 1.5x–3x throughput uplift at no quality cost. EAGLE-2 in vLLM, Medusa/tree-based in SGLang, ReDrafter in TensorRT-LLM.
- **DeepSeek V3/V4, Llama 4, Qwen3, GLM-5, Mistral Medium 3.5** all have day-0 support in SGLang and vLLM.
- **AMD MI355X** support added to SGLang — arguably better than vLLM's AMD support.
- **TGI (Text Generation Inference)** is effectively in maintenance mode; new optimizations land in vLLM/SGLang first.
